## About Me

I am **Xijing Wang**, a graduate student in the **Master of Science in Automated Science (MSAS)** program at **Carnegie Mellon University**, focusing on machine learning, robotics, and automation. I am affiliated with the **Ray and Stephanie Lane Computational Biology Department** in the **School of Computer Science**.

I completed my B.S. in Computer Science (Economics minor) at **Santa Clara University**. My research interests include:

- Machine learning and deep learning applications 
- Machine learning systems and infrastructure
- AI-driven automation and robotics
- Scalable and privacy-aware full-stack systems
